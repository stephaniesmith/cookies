Pat's Salmon Cookies

About

Pat's Salmon Cookies opened its first location in Seattle and we are excited to expand our buisness to Portland!

So why put salmon in cookies?! One day I absentmindedly confused two different mixing bowls and the salmon made it's way into some cookie dough. Curiosity struck me so I popped the cookies in the oven and so these sweet and salty confections were born! My husband and kids were sceptical at first, but after I tricked them into eating some they were hooked.

All our salmon is wild and is sourced form the PNW and Alaska. We proudly brew Stumptown Coffee, keeping those PNW vibes alive.

Swimming upstream since 2013.
